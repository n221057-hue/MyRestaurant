About Food Haven

A destination for authentic flavors, thoughtful cooking, and warm hospitality.

Food Haven is a destination for authentic flavors, thoughtful cooking, and warm hospitality. We believe great food is not just about taste, but about the experience that comes with it. Every dish at Food Haven is prepared using fresh ingredients, carefully sourced and skillfully crafted by our experienced chefs. Our menu brings together traditional favorites and modern creations, offering something special for every guest. Whether you are joining us for a family meal, a casual gathering, or a special celebration, we focus on providing a comfortable atmosphere, attentive service, and food that leaves a lasting impression. At Food Haven, we take pride in consistency, quality, and care. From the kitchen to the table, our goal is simple — to make every visit enjoyable, memorable, and worth returning for.

Arjun Mehta (head chef)

Education: Diploma in Culinary Arts – Institute of Hotel Management, Mumbai
Specialty: North Indian & Mughlai Cuisine
Experience: 12+ years

Expert in authentic Indian cuisine with over 12 years of experience, known for rich flavors and consistent quality.

Ananya Sharma (Pastry chef)

Education: Diploma in Baking & Pastry – Academy of Pastry Arts, Bangalore
Specialty: Desserts, Cakes & Fusion Sweets
Experience: 7 years

Creates elegant desserts and fusion sweets with precision, adding a sweet finish to every meal.

Rohan Kapoor (Grill chef)

Education: Advanced Culinary Training – Delhi Culinary Academy
Specialty: Tandoor, Grilled Meats & Starters
Experience: 10 years

Master of tandoor and grilled dishes, recognized for bold flavors and perfectly cooked starters.

Neha Rao (Sous chef)

Education: Bachelor’s in Hotel Management – Bengaluru
Specialty: South Indian & Continental Dishes
Experience: 9 years

Specializes in South Indian and continental dishes, bringing creativity and refined presentation to every plate.

Fresh & Quality Ingredients

We prioritize freshness and quality to ensure every dish meets high standards.

Customer- Service
